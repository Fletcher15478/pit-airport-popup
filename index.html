CHAD’S VANILLA

Chef Chad, co‑founder of Millie’s, is a firm believer that an ice‑cream maker is only as good as their vanilla ice cream. With no caramel or brownies or jam to hide behind, our vanilla ice cream is a simple, honest creation: just sweet cream & real vanilla bean.

BEST CHOCOLATE

Classic chocolate ice cream done the RIGHT way: we melt nuggets of 64% dark chocolate into our cream to create an intensely infused mix. The result is a deeply comforting chocolate ice cream that takes you straight back to a simpler time.

COOKIES ’N’ CREAM

Since its debut in the early eighties, this star flavor has become an ice‑cream parlor staple. With clean label cookies and a sweet vanilla ice cream, this classic combo is sure to satisfy every sweet tooth — even on‑the‑go!

MINT CHIP

Flecked with dark chocolate & made with real peppermint, our Dairy‑Free Mint Chip is an allergen‑friendly version of the American favorite. It’s everything you expect from this scoop‑shop classic, made without any artificial dyes or flavors — don’t miss this one.
